The brain is a compact survival machine, where 10 sensory inputs feed into 2 hidden sigmoid layers with a softmax activation over 8 movement directions, trained online by a health-delta reward signal using momentum SGD without true backprop. Intelligence is emergent from selection pressure, such that animals that navigate toward food/water and away from predators stay healthier, get rewarded, and reproduce. Their offspring inherit the architecture with fresh weights, carrying forward evolved scalar traits like speed and vision range.
