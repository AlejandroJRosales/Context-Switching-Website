Chern Class

Chern Class of Line Bundles

Chern classes are apart of algebraic topolgy, as well as other math groups, and are characterstic classes related to complex vector bundles. Let \(X\) be a topological space of closure-finite weak CW complex and let \(V\) be a line bundle. The first chern class is the only nontrivial Chern class and is an element of the second cohomology group of \(X\).
