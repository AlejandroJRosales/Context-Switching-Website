Poincaré Patch on Einstein Cylinder

Let \(Z^{I}\) with index \(I=-1,-,...,4\) be coordinates on embedding space \(R^{2,4}\) where the light cone of origin \(\mathbb{R}^{2,4}\) is cut out by:

\(\left(Z^{-1}\right)^2+\left(Z^0\right)^2=\left(Z^1\right)^2+\left(Z^2\right)^2+\left(Z^3\right)^2+\left(Z^4\right)^2\)

where the origin \(Z^{I}=0\) is removed. The remaining light code is postively rescaled with quotient \(Z^I \sim t Z^I, t \in \mathbb{R}_{+}\), arriving at Penroe's conformal compactification of \(\mathbb{M}\).
