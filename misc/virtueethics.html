Virtue Ethics

Introduction

Virtue Ethics is a branch of one of three major approahces to normative ehtics, where normative ethics, at the risk of over simplification, is concerened with criterias for what is right and wrong. Virtue ethics can be indentified from the other approaches to normative ethics, as that which emphasizes virutes or moral character. Whereas for deontology, another normative ethic approach, emphasizes duties or rules. The third normative approach emphasies the consequences of actions, which is consequentialism.
