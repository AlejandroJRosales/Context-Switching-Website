Why a Blog

I love teaching in depth about whatever new topic is that I am learning about! This website I coded provides a space for me to do that. Now, what I am learning about changes from week to week or month to month. Sometimes it is computer science, math, neuroscience, etc. I switch from topic to topic at will. Accordingly, this blog reflects that scatterbrained passion. Hence, the name of the website, context switching. I am writing this blog for educational purposes, yes, but mainly because it makes me happy to teach what I am learning in a way that is enjoyable to me as well! Hope you like it here!!
