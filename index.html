The name Context Switching was choosen for several reasons. Lets list them, in no order of importance. Context Switching occurs in a proccesor and the brain, my two favorite things. It is the act of switching back and forth between mutliple processes really fast giving the illusion of multitasking. This happens in a processor, but this also happens in the human brain. (One of the reasons why you cant listen to TV fully and listen to someone speak fully at the same time) But, this is what I feel like my passions/thoughts do as well. Swithcing between multiple subjects I am interested in really fast; switching between contexts or multitasking between categories. Now, since my passions and thoughts context switch, my conversations from start to end do as well. Jumping from topic to topic trying to teach and talk about about my newest passion. This can get annoying and confusing. So the people around me told me to get a blog. So here we are.
